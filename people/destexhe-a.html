About

Alain Destexhe served as a Principal Investigator at the Mainen Lab, where his research focused on kinetic models of synapses and channels, as well as spike generation and reliability. His work contributed to the lab's computational approaches to understanding neural circuit dynamics at the cellular and synaptic level.
