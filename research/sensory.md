How does the brain construct a perceptual experience from raw sensation? In collaboration with Noam Sobel's group at the Weizmann Institute, we study flavor perception -- running psychophysics experiments that probe how people discriminate and describe tastes, and what that reveals about the architecture of perceptual categories. This work addresses a fundamental question: whether perception is a direct readout of sensory input or an active construction shaped by expectation and context.
