Standard laboratory tasks capture a narrow slice of what brains actually do. We study human cognition in natural settings -- through ecological experiments, psychophysics outside the booth, and collaborations with artists that use installations and performances as experimental platforms. The goal is to develop methods and theory adequate to perception and decision-making as they occur in the uncontrolled complexity of real environments.
