Systems Neuroscience Lab

Champalimaud Foundation, Lisbon
We study how the brain makes decisions and learns from experience, with a focus on serotonin and other neuromodulators that reshape neural computation across the whole brain. Our current work spans large-scale neural recordings in mice, the neuroscience of psychedelics, and decoding human behavior from naturalistic signals. We're based at the Champalimaud Research Centre in Lisbon.
