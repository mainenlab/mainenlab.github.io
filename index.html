Mainen Lab — Systems Neuroscience

Champalimaud Foundation, Lisbon
The Mainen Lab at the Champalimaud Foundation investigates perception, space, learning & memory, decision & confidence, and consciousness. The lab currently has 15 active members — 1 PI, 4 postdocs, 4 PhD students, 1 MSc student, 4 technicians — working across 23 active research projects using behavior, electrophysiology, eye tracking, and optogenetics in mouse and human.
